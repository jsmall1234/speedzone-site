Questions

Where does it work?

Camera coverage currently includes San Francisco, Oakland, Los Angeles, Chicago, Washington D.C., Baltimore, and Malibu — over 460 cameras, with more cities rolling out. And the app runs everywhere: even outside a covered city your co-pilot still rides — or walks — along, so plenty of people use it just for the company. We're expanding coverage constantly.

Does it work when I'm walking?

Yes — flip to Walk mode and your co-pilot keeps you company on foot, with walking-style compliments and walking directions. You can set a target pace and it coaches you to hold it. (Speed-camera alerts are driving-only, since they don't apply to pedestrians.)

Does it track my walks?

If you want it to. Set a target pace and SpeedZone Baby coaches you to keep it, then saves the workout — distance, time, and pace — to Apple Health. You choose whether to grant Health access, and you're always in control of what's shared.

Is this legal?

Yes. SpeedZone Baby helps you stay aware of posted enforcement zones, the same way navigation apps do. It's a driver-awareness tool — always obey posted speed limits and traffic laws.

What about my privacy?

Your location is processed entirely on your device and is never sent to us. No accounts, no tracking, no ads, no data sold. Read the full privacy policy.

Can I turn off the flirting?

Absolutely. Choose safety-only during setup or flip the companion off anytime in Settings. The speed-zone alerts keep working either way.

Always obey posted speed limits and traffic laws. Alerts are informational only, and camera data may be incomplete or out of date.
