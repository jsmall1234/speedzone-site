Questions

Where does it work?

Coverage currently includes San Francisco and Oakland, with the Los Angeles County area rolling out soon. The app runs everywhere — you just won't hear camera alerts outside covered areas yet. We're expanding.

Is this legal?

Yes. SpeedZone Baby helps you stay aware of posted enforcement zones, the same way navigation apps do. It's a driver-awareness tool — always obey posted speed limits and traffic laws.

What about my privacy?

Your location is processed entirely on your device and is never sent to us. No accounts, no tracking, no ads, no data sold. Read the full privacy policy.

Can I turn off the flirting?

Absolutely. Choose safety-only during setup or flip the companion off anytime in Settings. The speed-zone alerts keep working either way.

Always obey posted speed limits and traffic laws. Alerts are informational only, and camera data may be incomplete or out of date.
